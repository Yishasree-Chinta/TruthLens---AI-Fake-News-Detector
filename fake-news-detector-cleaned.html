How TruthLens Works

A multi-layered AI analysis pipeline trained on thousands of verified and debunked stories.

01
Language Pattern Analysis
Identifies sensationalist language, hyperbole, excessive capitalization, and emotionally charged vocabulary designed to bypass critical thinking.
02
Claim Extraction
Breaks the article into individual factual claims and evaluates each one for internal consistency and logical coherence.
03
Source Credibility Signals
Evaluates citation patterns, specificity of claims, attribution quality, and common red flags used in misinformation campaigns.
04
Bias & Framing Detection
Detects political framing, selective omission, false equivalence, and manipulative narrative structures embedded in the text.
